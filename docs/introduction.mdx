# Why this Page and What are we going to Learn

Welcome to the AI-DevOps Learning Hub! This space is dedicated to all the infrastructure engineers and operations professionals who are curious about the world of Artificial Intelligence and want to leverage its power to automate, optimize, and revolutionize their daily workflows.

The goal of this hub is to provide a clear and concise learning path for individuals with a background in infrastructure to dive into AI. We will explore how to integrate AI into your daily life cycle, moving from manual processes to intelligent, automated systems.

We are going to learn how to start AI learning for Infra Engineers and Implement the use case into Daily Life Cycle. We will cover a range of topics from the fundamental concepts of prompt engineering to building and deploying AI agents and workflows. This journey is designed to be practical, hands-on, and directly applicable to the challenges you face in your day-to-day work.

Let's embark on this exciting learning adventure together!
